Settings: Status Wall

Follow Twitter hashtags

You can subscribe to Twitter hashtags, and tweets containing them will show up in your Status Wall. This will allow you to monitor a conversation on a given topic (hashtag) along with other news in ProSolo. You do not have to have a Twitter account nor need to connect your existing Twitter account with ProSolo in order to enable this functionality.

NOTE: too generic hashtags will pull in lots of tweets to your Status Wall.

Hashtags:
HINT: write hashtags without the hash sign (#) and separated with a comma (,). ProSolo will import the tweets with these hashtags to your Status Wall.
